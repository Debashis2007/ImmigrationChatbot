# Immigration Support Chatbot - Master Requirement

## Problem Statement
Build a chatbot that helps users facing immigration and career uncertainty by reducing stress, improving clarity, and improving communication quality with stakeholders.

## Core User Pain Points
1. **Waiting on slow or uncertain processes**
   - Visa filings, job offers, and approvals are unpredictable.
   - Users cannot confidently plan life decisions because timelines are unclear.
2. **Information overload without clarity**
   - Users see conflicting guidance on immigration rules, career moves, and salary expectations.
   - Excessive and inconsistent information causes confusion and mental fatigue.
3. **Communication gaps in important situations**
   - Users struggle to explain constraints (visa needs, work arrangements) to employers, recruiters, and institutions.
   - Small misunderstandings can cause large consequences.

## Product Goal
Provide a trustworthy AI assistant that:
- Synthesizes complex information into clear, actionable guidance.
- Helps users track uncertain processes and plan next steps.
- Drafts clear communication messages tailored to context.

## Scope (MVP)
- Conversational assistant for immigration/career support.
- Timeline planning support with uncertainty-aware guidance.
- Evidence-based answers with confidence level and source notes.
- Message drafting for employer/recruiter/institution communication.

## Out of Scope (MVP)
- Legal representation or legally binding advice.
- Automatic filing submission to external government systems.
- Guaranteed predictions on approval dates.

## High-Level Success Criteria
- User receives a structured response with: summary, assumptions, next actions.
- User receives at least one communication draft for selected scenario.
- User can ask follow-up questions to refine plan without restarting context.
- Assistant clearly indicates uncertainty and confidence when data is incomplete.
